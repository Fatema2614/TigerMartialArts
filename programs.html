Explore Our Programs


At Tiger Martial Arts, we take pride in offering a wide range of martial arts classes tailored for individuals of all ages and experience levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned practitioner, our programs are designed to help you grow physically, mentally, and emotionally. From the disciplined techniques of Karate and the grappling mastery of Judo to the striking power of Muay Thai and our dynamic Fitness Training sessions, there's something for everyone. We also offer dedicated Kids' programs across various martial arts styles, focusing on building discipline, confidence, coordination, and respect in a safe and encouraging environment. Our Self-Defense courses are ideal for those seeking practical techniques for real-world scenarios, while our adult programs emphasize both tradition and modern training approaches. At Tiger Martial Arts, we believe in creating a supportive community where every student can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Programs

Karate

Karate offers a traditional and disciplined path to physical and mental development. Students will learn powerful striking techniques, including punches, kicks, blocks, and stances, all rooted in strong foundational movements. Our Karate program promotes personal growth, self-control, and respect for others, while enhancing strength, agility, and focus. It's ideal for those looking to boost self-confidence and gain practical self-defense skills.

Jiu-Jitsu

Our Jiu-Jitsu classes focus on the powerful principles of leverage and technique over brute strength. This program emphasizes ground fighting, joint locks, and submission holds—making it an ideal martial art for self-defense and real-world scenarios. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced grappler, our structured classes cater to all levels. You'll not only improve your physical fitness but also gain confidence, discipline, and a deep understanding of body mechanics and strategy.

Judo

The Judo program introduces students to the art of throws, pins, and ground control. Derived from traditional Japanese martial arts, Judo teaches how to use an opponent's energy and movement to gain the upper hand. Classes are designed to improve balance, timing, and awareness, while instilling respect and humility. Great for both self-defense and sport, our Judo sessions help develop resilience and mental toughness.

Kid's Karate

Our Kids Karate classes are energetic, interactive, and full of learning opportunities. Young students are taught respect, focus, and perseverance through structured routines and martial arts principles. With an emphasis on safety and discipline, children learn effective striking techniques while building coordination, social skills, and a strong sense of self-worth. Progress is tracked through belt levels, encouraging goal setting and achievement.

Kid's Jiu-Jitsu

Specially tailored for younger students, our Kids Jiu-Jitsu program is designed to build confidence, coordination, and discipline in a fun and safe environment. With a strong focus on technique, teamwork, and anti-bullying principles, children learn the fundamentals of grappling while improving their motor skills and self-esteem. Our qualified instructors ensure that every class balances structured learning with engaging, age-appropriate drills.

Kid's Judo

Kids Judo focuses on introducing children to grappling in a controlled, fun, and supportive environment. They'll learn basic throws, falls, and holds, improving their physical coordination and self-confidence. Emphasis is placed on teamwork, respect for others, and personal discipline—making it an excellent foundation for both martial arts and life.

Muay Thai

Known as the “Art of Eight Limbs,” Muay Thai is a dynamic striking art that uses punches, kicks, elbows, and knees. Our classes are intense, energetic, and designed to build endurance, agility, and strength. Ideal for fitness enthusiasts and competitive fighters alike, this program focuses on technique, power, and mental focus. It's a full-body workout that also teaches practical and effective striking for self-defense.

Fitness Training

This non-combat program is all about conditioning your body and improving your overall health. Our Fitness Training classes include cardio workouts, strength training, flexibility exercises, and martial arts-inspired drills. Whether you’re looking to lose weight, boost endurance, or complement your martial arts practice, these sessions are designed to help you reach your fitness goals in a high-energy, motivational environment.

Self-Defense

Our Self-Defense Courses provide practical techniques and strategies for real-world situations. Open to everyone, regardless of age or fitness level, these classes focus on awareness, prevention, and quick-response tactics. Participants learn how to handle common threats using effective strikes, escapes, and defensive maneuvers. Ideal for individuals seeking to feel more empowered, safe, and prepared in everyday life.
