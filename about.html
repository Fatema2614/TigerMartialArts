The Birth of tiger martial arts


Tiger Martial Arts was founded in 2012 with a simple but powerful belief — that martial arts is more than just combat; it's a way of life that builds character, discipline, confidence, and community. Our story began in a small studio with a handful of dedicated students and a passionate instructor who wanted to make martial arts accessible to everyone — regardless of age, gender, or experience level. With a focus on traditional values and modern training techniques, Tiger Martial Arts quickly became a respected name in the local community. As word spread, our dojo expanded — not just in space, but in spirit. We introduced new programs like Kids Karate, Muay Thai, Jiu-Jitsu, and Self-Defense Courses to meet the needs of our growing student base. We've proudly seen students grow from shy beginners into confident black belts, and even future instructors. Through the years, we've stayed true to our core values — respect, perseverance, humility, and self-control. Whether you're here to learn self-defense, improve fitness, build focus, or just find your inner warrior, Tiger Martial Arts is a place where you belong.


Our Vision


At Tiger Martial Arts, our vision is to become a leading martial arts academy recognized for transforming lives through confidence, discipline, and resilience. We believe in empowering individuals — from children to adults — to face life’s challenges with strength, focus, and honor. Our goal is to create a future where everyone, regardless of background or ability, can discover their potential through the power and philosophy of martial arts.

Our Mission


Our mission is to provide high-quality, inclusive martial arts training in a safe, respectful, and motivating environment. We are committed to promoting physical fitness, mental clarity, and emotional well-being through structured and disciplined practice. Beyond just techniques, we strive to build a strong community that values growth, support, and personal achievement. At Tiger Martial Arts, we guide each student to not only develop their skills but to become respectful, confident individuals both inside and outside the dojo.

Our Founders


Sensai Marcus Tanaka

A 5th-degree black belt in Karate and a former national competitor, Sensei Marcus brings over 20 years of experience in teaching and competing. He leads with discipline and precision, emphasizing traditional martial arts values and personal excellence.

Coach Lena Alvarez

With a background in Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai, Coach Lena is known for her dynamic training style and motivational coaching. Her focus is on empowering students through self-defense and mental toughness, especially young women and teens.

Master Jamal Reed

A lifelong martial artist and certified instructor in multiple disciplines including Judo and Kickboxing, Master Jamal is the heart of the dojo. He’s known for his mentorship and community outreach, always striving to make martial arts accessible to all.
